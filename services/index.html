Frequently asked questions

What to expect.

Which platforms do you support?

The mix depends on your audience and business model. Support can be shaped around platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook and Instagram where they are commercially relevant.

Do you create the posts?

Yes. Support can include planning, post ideas, copywriting and campaign themes, depending on the agreed scope.

Will you promise a specific number of followers?

No. The focus is on building relevant visibility, trust and useful engagement rather than chasing numbers that may have little commercial value.

Do we need to post every day?

No. A realistic, consistent rhythm is usually more valuable than a high volume of rushed or repetitive content.
