How it works

What a partnership actually looks like.

Every partnership starts with a kickoff session to agree what matters most. From there, work happens on a weekly or fortnightly rhythm, depending on what is required.

01
Kickoff and priorities

We agree the commercial priorities, immediate needs and what success should look like.

02
Regular check-ins

A short weekly or fortnightly call keeps priorities clear and decisions moving.

03
Ongoing delivery

Work continues between calls, with clear visibility of what is being done and why.

04
Monthly or quarterly review

Priorities are revisited as the business changes, rather than being fixed for a year and forgotten.

There is no account team, no handover and no need to re-explain your business to someone new. The person doing the strategic thinking is the same person delivering the work.

Frequently asked questions

What to expect.

Is there a long-term contract?

No 12-month commitment is required. The working arrangement and notice period are agreed clearly before the partnership begins.

How much time is included?

The level of support depends on the agreed priorities and scope. The starting price is £1,025 per month, with the arrangement shaped around what the business actually needs.

Will you work with our existing team or suppliers?

Yes. Partnership can include supporting internal teams, coordinating specialists or giving clearer direction to existing suppliers.

What if we are not ready for ongoing support?

The Marketing MOT may be a better starting point if you first need clarity on where to focus and what should happen next.
