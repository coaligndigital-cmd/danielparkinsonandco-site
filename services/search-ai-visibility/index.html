Frequently asked questions

What to expect.

What is the difference between SEO, AEO and GEO?

SEO improves visibility in search engines. AEO focuses on being surfaced as a useful answer. GEO focuses on how content is understood and referenced by generative AI systems.

Can you guarantee first-page rankings?

No. No credible provider can guarantee rankings. The focus is on improving the quality, clarity and authority signals that support sustainable visibility.

Do I need a completely new website?

Not necessarily. Many visibility improvements can be made within an existing site by strengthening structure, content and technical foundations.

How quickly will results appear?

Some technical or local improvements can have an early effect, while broader organic visibility usually builds over time. The pace depends on your market, site history and competition.
