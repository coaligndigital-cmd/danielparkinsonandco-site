Frequently asked questions

What to expect.

Which email platforms do you work with?

Support can be adapted to commonly used platforms. The first step is to review what you already have and whether it remains suitable for your needs.

Do you write the emails?

Yes. Support can include planning, copywriting, building campaigns and coordinating approvals, depending on the agreed scope.

Do we need a large mailing list?

No. A smaller, relevant list can be more valuable than a large disengaged one. The focus is on useful communication and quality relationships.

Can you help with GDPR and consent?

I can help you structure the marketing process and highlight practical considerations, but formal legal advice should come from a qualified data-protection adviser where needed.
