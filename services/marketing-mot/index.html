Frequently asked questions

What to expect.

Is this only a website review?

No. Your website is part of the review, but the Marketing MOT looks more broadly at messaging, channels, content, customer journeys and how your marketing supports the business.

Will you need access to our accounts?

Where available, access to analytics, email or social platforms can provide useful context. We will agree what is needed before the review begins.

Do I have to buy ongoing support afterwards?

No. The Marketing MOT is a standalone service. You can implement the recommendations yourself or with your existing team.

What happens if more support would help?

I will explain the options honestly. That might mean a focused service, an ongoing Partnership or simply giving your team clearer direction.
