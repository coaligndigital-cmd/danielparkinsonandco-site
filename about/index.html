How I work

Senior thinking, delivered directly.

Most engagements start the same way: a conversation about what's actually going on in the business, not a sales pitch about what I can sell you.

From there, I typically:

  • Look at what's already happening — your website, channels, messaging, and what's actually converting versus what just looks busy.
  • Agree the two or three things that matter most — not a twenty-point plan nobody will action.
  • Get on with the work myself — I don't hand your brief to a junior or an offshore team. If I'm doing the strategy, I'm also doing the delivery.
  • Report on what actually moved — not numbers that just look good in a slide.

Some clients bring me in for a single fixed-scope piece — a Marketing MOT, a website review. Others keep me on for ongoing support, working alongside them for as long as they need it — without the cost or commitment of a full-time hire.

Either way, the approach is the same: senior thinking, applied directly, with nothing lost in translation between the person deciding and the person doing.
