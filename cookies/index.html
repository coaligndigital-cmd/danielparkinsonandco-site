Cookie Policy

How Daniel Parkinson & Co. uses cookies.

This page explains the essential and optional technologies used on the website, what they do and how you can manage your preferences.

Start a conversation Back to home

Last updated: July 2026

Daniel Parkinson & Co. uses a small number of cookies and similar browser-storage technologies to make the website work properly and, where you consent, to understand how visitors use it.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies, such as local storage, can also remember choices or help provide website functionality.

How cookies are used

The website uses two broad categories of cookies or similar technologies:

Category Purpose Consent required?
Essential Supports basic website functions and remembers whether you accepted or declined optional analytics. No
Analytics Helps understand which pages are visited, how people move through the site and how the website performs. Yes

Essential cookies and storage

Essential technologies are used to support core functionality. This includes remembering your cookie preference so the website does not repeatedly ask you to make the same choice.

These technologies do not require consent where they are strictly necessary for the website to function as expected.

Analytics cookies

If you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics may be used to collect information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Approximate time spent on pages
  • Device and browser type
  • General location information
  • How visitors arrived at the website

This information is used in aggregate to understand website performance and improve content and navigation.

Analytics should only load after consent.

The website is intended to load optional analytics only when you select “Accept” in the cookie banner.

Managing your preferences

When you first visit the website, you can accept or decline optional analytics cookies using the cookie banner.

You can reset your choice by clearing cookies and site data for dpandco.co.uk in your browser settings, then revisiting the website.

Browser controls

Most browsers allow you to view, block or delete cookies and site data. The exact steps depend on your browser and device.

Blocking all cookies or browser storage may prevent some essential website features from working correctly.

Third-party services

Where analytics are accepted, Google Analytics may place or read cookies and process website-usage information in accordance with Google's own privacy practices.

Changes to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the website, the tools used or relevant legal requirements. The latest version will always be published on this page.

More information

For information about how personal information is handled, read the Privacy Policy.

To ask a question about cookies or website analytics, please use the contact form.
